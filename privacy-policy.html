Privacy Policy

Simora Paper Trader Extension

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

Activation Data

  • Activation codes: Used solely for access control and license verification
  • Device identifiers: Generated locally to prevent code sharing
  • Platform usage: Which supported websites you use the extension on

Local Data

  • Trading data: All your paper trading positions, transactions, and performance data
  • Settings: Your preferences and configuration settings
  • Cache data: Temporary price and market data for functionality

How We Use Information

Activation System

  • Verify your access rights to use the extension
  • Prevent unauthorized usage and code sharing
  • Provide technical support when needed

Local Functionality

  • Store your trading data locally in your browser
  • Provide real-time trading simulation
  • Generate performance reports and analytics

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All trading data is stored locally in your browser
  • No trading data is transmitted to our servers
  • Data remains private and under your control

Server Communication

  • Activation only: We only communicate with our servers for license verification
  • No personal data: We don't collect names, emails, or personal information
  • Secure connections: All communications use HTTPS encryption

Data Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your trading information
  • Track your browsing habits
  • Use analytics or advertising networks

We MAY share data only:

  • When required by law
  • To prevent fraud or abuse of our service

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data: Uninstall the extension to remove all local data
  • Request information: Contact us about your activation status
  • Revoke access: Request deactivation of your code

Third-Party Services

This extension interacts with:

  • GMGN.ai: For price data and trading interface
  • Axiom.trade: For price data and trading interface
  • Our license server: For activation verification only

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions: simoralabs@gmail.com

Note: This extension is for educational paper trading only. No real money or financial transactions are involved.
